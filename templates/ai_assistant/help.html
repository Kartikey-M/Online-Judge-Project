AI Assistant Help

What is the AI Assistant?

Our AI Assistant is powered by Google's Gemini AI and is designed to help you learn and improve your programming skills. It provides intelligent hints and debugging suggestions without giving away complete solutions.

Features
  • Problem Hints: Get helpful hints for any problem that guide you toward the solution without spoiling it
  • Wrong Answer Analysis: Understand why your solution might be producing incorrect results
  • Runtime Error Help: Get suggestions for fixing runtime errors in your code
  • Time Limit Analysis: Learn how to optimize your code when it exceeds time limits
How to Use
  1. Getting Hints: On any problem page, click the "Get AI Hint" button to receive a helpful hint
  2. Analyzing Submissions: When your submission fails, you'll see an "Analyze with AI" button to get debugging suggestions
  3. Learning from Feedback: Use the AI's suggestions to understand concepts and improve your approach
Best Practices
  • Try to solve problems on your own first before asking for hints
  • Use hints as learning opportunities, not shortcuts
  • Read the AI's analysis carefully and try to understand the underlying concepts
  • Apply the lessons learned to similar problems
Note: The AI Assistant is designed to help you learn, not to provide complete solutions. It will guide you toward the right approach while encouraging you to think through the problem yourself.
